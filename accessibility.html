Accessibility

Accessibility statement

I want this site to be usable by as many people as possible, including visitors who rely on assistive technology. It is built with accessibility in mind and aims to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA and the Israeli standard IS 5568.

What's in place

  • Semantic HTML landmarks and a logical heading structure.
  • Descriptive alternative text on all meaningful images.
  • Full keyboard operation, with a visible focus indicator and a "skip to content" link.
  • Text colour contrast that meets AA (a ratio of 4.5:1 or better).
  • Respect for the "reduce motion" system setting, so animations are minimised for those who prefer it.
  • A responsive layout that works down to small mobile screens and supports text zoom.

Known limitations

This portfolio links out to third-party platforms (YouTube, Instagram, and B&G Guitars) whose content and accessibility are outside my control.

Found a problem?

If you run into any accessibility barrier on this site, please tell me and I'll do my best to fix it promptly.
