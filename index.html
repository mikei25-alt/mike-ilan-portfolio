Step Sister · Golden Camphor A 250% ROI launch, sold out in two weeks.

A premium product launch, owned end to end: strategy, story, shoot, page, and spend.

I defined the positioning, wrote all the product copy and the page spec, then partnered with our UI/UX designer on a bespoke layout and flow.
